Design sleep for thinking

A quiet sleep app for people who think for a living. See your sleep as a calm read on how clearly you'll think tomorrow — never a verdict.

What it does

  • Log bedtime and wake time with a quick subjective rating
  • A sleep score and sleep-debt view — a guide, not a judgement
  • A recovery-aware bedtime reminder, worked back from your goal
  • Daily, weekly and monthly trends for duration and quality

Private by design

All data stays on your device. No account, no sign-in, no tracking, no ads.

すべてのデータは端末内に保存。アカウント登録・ログイン・トラッキングはありません。
